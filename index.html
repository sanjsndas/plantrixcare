Our Story

Founded in 2015 by a group of horticulture enthusiasts, Plantrix Care began as a small neighborhood service with a simple mission: to provide sustainable irrigation solutions for urban environments. Our founders, all graduates of Cornell University's School of Integrative Plant Science, recognized the growing need for efficient plant care solutions in New York City's dense urban landscape.

What started as a modest operation servicing just a few blocks in Greenwich Village has grown into New York's premier residential plant care provider, now serving all five boroughs. Our team has expanded to include over 50 certified horticulturists, irrigation specialists, and plant care technicians, each bringing their unique expertise to our comprehensive service offerings.

We combine cutting-edge irrigation technology with time-tested plant care techniques to ensure your greenery thrives in even the most challenging urban conditions. Our proprietary Plantrix Water Optimization System (PWOS) has been recognized by the Urban Horticulture Society for its innovative approach to water conservation while maintaining plant health.

Our Mission

At Plantrix Care, we're committed to revolutionizing urban plant care through sustainable practices, technological innovation, and deep horticultural knowledge. Plant Care Tips

Simple tips to keep your plants healthy and thriving

Watering

Always check the soil moisture before watering. Overwatering can be just as harmful as underwatering. Use a moisture meter for accurate readings.

Sunlight

Different plants have different light requirements. Make sure to place your plants in the right spot, considering their specific needs for sunlight exposure.

Fertilizing

Use organic fertilizers to provide essential nutrients without harming the environment. Follow the recommended schedule for your specific plant types.

Pruning

Regular pruning helps promote healthy growth and prevents overcrowding. Learn the best techniques for each plant species to ensure optimal results.

Pest Control

Monitor your plants regularly for signs of pests. Use natural remedies or consult with our experts for effective pest management solutions.

Seasonal Care

Adjust your care routine with the changing seasons. Different plants have varying needs throughout the year, so stay informed and adapt accordingly.
