Send Us a Message

1. General Inquiries

For general questions about our services, products, or company, please feel free to contact us using the form above. Our customer service team is available Monday through Friday from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM EST to assist you. We strive to respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days.

2. Customer Support

Having issues with our products or services? Our dedicated customer support team is here to help you resolve any problems quickly and efficiently. Please provide as much detail as possible about your issue so we can serve you better. For immediate assistance, call our support line at (212) 555-0199 during business hours.

3. Technical Support

For technical questions about our smart irrigation systems or other technical products, our specialized technical support team is available to assist you. Please be ready with your product model number and a description of the issue you're experiencing when contacting us. Technical support is available via phone, email, or live chat on our website.

4. Sales Questions

Interested in our products or services? Our sales team can provide detailed information about pricing, features, and implementation. We can schedule a free consultation to assess your needs and recommend the best solutions. For large-scale commercial inquiries, please contact our business development department at sales@plantrixcare.com.

5. Billing Questions

For questions about billing, invoices, or payment methods, please contact our billing department at billing@plantrixcare.com or call (212) 555-0199 ext. 2. Have your account number ready for faster service. Our billing specialists are available Monday through Friday from 8:00 AM to 4:00 PM EST to assist with all billing-related inquiries.

6. Account Management

Need help with your online account? Our account management team can assist with password resets, account updates, and access issues. For security reasons, some account changes may require verification of identity. You can manage many account settings yourself by logging into your account dashboard on our website.

7. Service Scheduling

To schedule or reschedule a service appointment, please use our online scheduling tool or call our service center at (212) 555-0199 ext. 3. We recommend scheduling at least one week in advance, though we may be able to accommodate last-minute requests depending on availability. Same-day emergency service may be available for an additional fee.

8. Existing Service Issues

If you're experiencing issues with an ongoing service, please contact our service department immediately at service@plantrixcare.com or call (212) 555-0199 ext. 4. Be prepared to provide your service contract number and details about the problem. Our goal is to resolve all service issues within 48 hours of notification.

9. Supply Orders

To order plant care supplies or replacement parts, please visit our online store or contact our supplies department at supplies@plantrixcare.com. Bulk orders may qualify for special pricing discounts. Most standard orders shipped within the continental U.S. arrive within 3-5 business days. Expedited shipping options are available.

10. Installation Questions

Have questions about installing your Plantrix irrigation system? Our installation support team can guide you through the process or arrange professional installation. DIY installation guides are available for download on our website. For complex installations, we strongly recommend using our certified installation technicians for optimal performance.

11. Product Registration

Register your Plantrix products to activate your warranty and receive important updates. You can register products online through our website or by completing and mailing in the product registration card included with your purchase. Registered products may qualify for extended warranty coverage and premium support services.

12. Warranty Claims

To file a warranty claim, please contact our warranty department at warranty@plantrixcare.com with your product details and a description of the issue. Most claims require proof of purchase and product registration. Our standard warranty covers defects in materials and workmanship for one year from date of purchase, with options for extended coverage.

13. Returns and Refunds

Our return policy allows most unused products to be returned within 30 days of purchase for a full refund. Please contact our returns department at returns@plantrixcare.com to initiate a return. Some items may be subject to restocking fees. Custom or special order items may not be returnable. Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after we receive the returned item.
